# Summary — Trader Performance vs Market Sentiment

## Methodology

The analysis involved cleaning and preprocessing trader and sentiment datasets, converting timestamps, merging datasets by date, and analyzing trader performance across different market sentiment conditions. Trader segmentation and predictive modeling were performed to identify behavioral patterns.

## Key Insights

Trader performance shows significant variation across market sentiment states. Traders exhibit higher risk-taking behavior during Greed markets and reduced exposure during Fear markets. The average win rate of traders is approximately 40%, indicating a high-risk environment.

## Strategy Recommendations

Traders should reduce risk exposure during Fear markets and increase trading activity cautiously during Greed markets.
